auxd MVP — Wireframes

Feature: 001-auxd-mvp · Phase: 2 · Fidelity: basic-html · 4 critical screens

About these wireframes: Basic HTML, layout-only. No final colors, type, or interaction polish — those are deferred to implementation. The four screens below are the load-bearing surfaces identified in user-journeys.md. Wireframes for secondary screens (profile, settings, search results) are deliberately omitted at this phase — they can be wireframed in Phase 5 or directly designed during implementation. The wedge interaction (Log sheet) is the highest-priority surface; everything else supports it.

1. Onboarding — "Pick critics to follow"

The activation screen. User has just signed up and picked a handle; we show 5 critic profiles and require at least 3 follows before they continue to the feed.

View wireframe

2. Home feed

The default home surface. Reverse-chronological with weighting; entry cards from followed users.

View wireframe

3. Log sheet (the wedge)

The bottom-sheet log/rate/review interaction. Sub-8-second commit target. THE wedge interaction.

View wireframe

4. Album detail page

Album cover, metadata, tracklist, friends-who-rated, public reviews. SSR'd for share-link previews.

View wireframe

Wireframes intentionally exclude: profile pages, settings pages, search results, backlog/Up Next, onboarding steps 1/2/4/5, empty states. These follow established patterns documented in research/ux-patterns.md and will be designed during implementation.
